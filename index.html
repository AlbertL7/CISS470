üìö About This Learning Portal

This interactive learning portal provides an engaging way to explore "The Cyber Risk Handbook: Creating and Measuring Effective Cybersecurity Capabilities" edited by Domenic Antonucci. Each chapter has been transformed into an interactive learning experience with scenarios, quizzes, and expandable content.

NIST Standards Integration: In addition to the handbook chapters, this portal includes comprehensive modules on critical NIST standards including FIPS 199 (Security Categorization) and NIST SP 800-53 (Security Controls). These modules connect federal standards to practical implementation.

How to Use: Click on any available chapter to begin. Each chapter is divided into sections that you can explore at your own pace. Interactive elements, knowledge checks, and practical scenarios help reinforce key concepts.

Navigation: Within each chapter, use the navigation buttons to move between sections, check your progress, and test your understanding with built-in quizzes.
